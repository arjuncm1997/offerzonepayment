About

This website is designed to integrate a number of selling outlets to combine and form a grouped organisation of a corporate. Anyone in the public can view to decide their purchase venue as described in the website. Any mall,hop, or product can be registered in the websoite by the order of mall, shop, product, offer. The websiste is designed to update frequently according to the new offers available for an item. Every offer is sorted according to the district. Anyone from the public can sbmit their questions and report an issue acccording to their convinience. Public viewer can use the dropdown list to search for the district he needs and thus the offers of theat district wil be available for hm.
